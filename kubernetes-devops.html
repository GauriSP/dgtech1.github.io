Kubernetes: Orchestrating DevOps Platforms

Kubernetes is the industry-standard platform for container orchestration and a core DevOps skill. It automates deployment, scaling, networking, and self-healing of containerized applications.

Kubernetes enables high availability, rolling updates, service discovery, and fault tolerance, making it ideal for microservices and cloud-native systems.

For DevOps professionals, Kubernetes expertise is essential for managing scalable, resilient workloads across on-premises, hybrid, and cloud environments.
